Service U

Service U is an open source project that we hope will help better connect students on college campuses. Service U has 5 main components. They are forums, a buy/sell page with categories, a jobs page, an events page, and anonynous forums that will connect students with counselors. In total, we are a team of two front end developers and one backend developer. I am a cofounder and the backend developer for the project.
